ABOUT US

“English language is a strange paradise. It’s where the fishes fly and the sparrows swim.”
The English Literary Society is a group of people who are bonded mainly, but not exclusively, by their love for English language and literature. The club’s environment comprises discussions set in the world of literature and provides a space where you are free to talk about the most bizarre ideas and curiosities that pop-up in your mind.
We at the ELS explore new methods for writing and perform at poetry slams. TV shows, novels, films, puns, cryptic crosswords: name your interest, and you will find like-minded people here.
Events like DumbC (our fun twist on charades) and Just-A-Minute ( Jammers, anyone?) are pure laughter and an exhibit for some serious talent. Word Games are an assortment of obsessive puzzles and sneaky humour based on the idiosyncrasies of the English language. We also organise the fresher's Treasure Hunt every year: a rite you don't want to miss.
Once in ELS, always in ELS. With all that said, in ELS you will find a welcoming, closely-knitted group of friends unimpeded by batch (or any!) differences. Looking forward to having fun with you!
